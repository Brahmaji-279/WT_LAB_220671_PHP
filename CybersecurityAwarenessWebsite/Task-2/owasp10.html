What is OWASP-TOP10?
      The OWASP Top 10 is a crucial awareness document listing the ten most critical security risks to web applications, compiled by the Open Web Application Security Project (OWASP) based on global expert consensus, helping developers and organizations prioritize secure coding and risk mitigation. The side document shows the owasp-top-10 vulnerbilities in the field of software engineering in the year of 2022.
